You may wonder how to use What is my endorsement level?

Simply go to http://whatismyendorsement.herokuapp.com/XX/B_TAG where XX is your platform (possible values pc, psn, xbl) and B_TAG is your battle.net battletag like Valkia-2299.

Few examples:

http://whatismyendorsement.herokuapp.com/pc/Valkia-2299

http://whatismyendorsement.herokuapp.com/psn/Mercy

http://whatismyendorsement.herokuapp.com/xbl/vent

Remember your battletag is case sensitive, so Valkia-2299 from examples above will work like a charm, but valkia-2299 not.

If you find any bug or battletag which is not working properly let me know here
